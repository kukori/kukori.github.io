Privacy Policy for LingoTap

Effective Date: August 5, 2025

1. Introduction

Attila Varjas E. V. ("we," "our," or "us") operates the mobile application, LingoTap (the "App"). We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This policy outlines our practices concerning the collection, use, and disclosure of information.

Our App is designed to function without collecting any personally identifiable information like your name, email address, or contacts. Your privacy is a top priority.

2. Information We Collect

While we do not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use our App, certain non-personal information may be collected automatically by the App and its integrated third-party services to ensure its functionality and for analytical purposes. This includes:

  • Usage Data: Anonymous information about your interaction with the App, such as features used and session duration. This helps us understand which features are popular and how we can improve the App.
  • Device and Technical Data: Information about your device, such as the operating system version, device model, and screen resolution. This is used to ensure compatibility and optimize the App for different devices.
  • Crash and Performance Data: If the App crashes, anonymous diagnostic data may be collected to help us identify and fix bugs.
